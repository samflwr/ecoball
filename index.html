Hardware Components

Precision engineering with eco‑conscious materials and calibrated sensors.

Outer Structure

  • Floating HDPE or recycled‑material sphere
  • Ergonomic, hydrodynamic design by Violo & Buzatu

Electronics / MCU

  • STM32 Nucleo
  • Professional multiparametric water sensors
  • High‑sensitivity GPS
  • NB‑IoT / LTE‑M / GSM / LoRa modem
  • ADC signal conditioning
  • Smart solar/battery management (simplified MPPT)

Power System

  • 10–20 Wh Li‑ion battery

  • Intelligent power‑saving modes
