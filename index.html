Who we are?


We are the last generation able to calm the continuing growth of pollution in the world, before it is too late. We thought that making a product capable of analyzing the state of pollution of waterways, rivers, lakes and seas could be useful in contributing to the fight against environmental degradation.

Currently, there is a tendency to analyze those areas which, due to their conformation and location, are considered to be at risk of pollution. Our project stems from the need to extend the detection of pollutants over larger areas, in order to generate a map of the areas at risk and to carry out preventive monitoring ensuring the possibility of targeted interventions.

The idea is based on the possibility of detecting water samples using a mobile detection device that carries out samples at regular intervals in different areas, communicating a set of data aimed at creating dynamic reports in real time. The device was built following all the criteria based on the concept of Green Energy, making itself autonomous as regards the power supply of its internal devices.
